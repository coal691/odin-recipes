Cole's Salmon

Yummy Cooked Salmon!

This recipe will be quick and super easy! I love this healthy and fresh salmon, and it's absolutely perfect once a week or every two weeks.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Ounces of Fresh Caught Wild Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 Lemon

How to Cook

  1. Turn on Your grill to a medium-high heat (400-425 Fahrenheit)
  2. While the grill heats up, place the salmon on a large bed of aluminum foil
  3. Generously season the top with salt and pepper
  4. Flip the salmon and generously season the bottom
  5. Flip the salmon again and season the top once more to your liking
  6. Cut the lemon and squeeze the juice generously on top of the salmon
  7. Wrap the salmon in a tent of aluminum foil (make sure it is sealed!)
  8. Place the salmon the grill for 12-15 minutes
  9. Remove the salmon and enjoy!

This was only a step by step guide on how to prepare the salmon. You can choose whatever you want to go along with it as a side dish!

My personal favorite side dishes with this are brown rice and cooked asparagus :)
